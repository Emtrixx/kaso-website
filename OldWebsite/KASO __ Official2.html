BIO

(View German Bio)

German artist, Kaso, uses a blend of R&B, Hip-Hop, Funk, Jazz, and Funk-Rock to craft his music. The young talent has been influenced by the dance style Krump, which manifests throughout his work. Kaso harnessed his creative abilities of singing, songwriting, dancing and poetry to make his music come into fruition.

By the age of 20, the artist began producing original content on his own. Having moved to Los Angeles, California three years later made another impact on his world view which has transpired into his music. Kasos strong sense of individuality has made him stand out in society in a way that his peers have noticed. In his youth, Kaso has been known for not fitting into the typical stereotype. He always stood out because of the artforms he drew inspiration from, the style he expressed and the way he carried himself. A fresh sound is what I want to bring to the world of art, Kaso says. I want to bring something new to the table.
